Editors

Ashley Haugen

Managing Editor

Ashley Haugen began her editorial career more than 20 years ago. With experience in both print and digital formats, it’s the speed at which the internet operates and the opportunity to learn about absolutely anything online that most intrigues her. With a communications degree from Boston University, Ashley has experience in a variety of verticals, including parenting, health/wellness, and women’s lifestyle.

Retha Lombard

Edito, History-Computer

Retha has been in love with computers and technology for as long as she can remember. After quite a few years as the Information Technology officer of a local municipality, followed by a stint as a high school teacher teaching Java programming, the next logical step was to be editor of one of the great up-and-coming technology sites. She really enjoys working with knowledgeable owners, fellow editors, and writers! When not busy on the site, Retha enjoys going to the beach with her grandchildren, and working and playing in the garden with her dogs.

Sarah Cavicchi

Editor, Moms Who Think

Sarah Cavicchi loves creating approachable and helpful health and wellness content and has more than a decade of digital content experience. She has previously worked with brands like Zappos, Colgate, and U.S. News and World Report. When she's not slinging SEO and storytelling best practices, Sarah enjoys exploring her hometown of Boston and the New England area with loved ones.

Will Axford

Editor, CheatCC

Will Axford lives in Houston, TX and is originally from Michigan. He once worked as a reporter in the Florida Keys writing about crime and endangered species. When not playing video games or editing, Will likes to listen to audiobooks, play D&D, and watch football.
